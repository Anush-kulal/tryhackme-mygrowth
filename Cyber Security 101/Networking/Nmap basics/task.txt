###NMAP Basic
Learn how to use nmap to discover live hosts,find open ports , and detect services version.

##Objectives of nmap
1.Discover live hosts
2.Find running services on the live hosts.
3.Distinguish the different types of port scan
4.Detect the version of running services.
5.Control the timing.
6.Formate the output.
