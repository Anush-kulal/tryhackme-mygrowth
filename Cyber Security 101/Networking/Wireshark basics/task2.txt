##Packet Dissection
It is the dissecting the packet with protocol dissection and some scripting for investigation and analysis.

Today i learnt like if i double click on any packet it will show the packet details and if i click on any protocol it will show the protocol details.
The below sub sections are the details of the packet.After double clicking on any packet it will show the packet details.
-Frame:It is at the layer 1 of OSI model.
-Source and Destination MAC address:It is at the layer 2 of OSI model.
-Source and Destination IP address:It is at the layer 3 of OSI model.
-Source and Destination port:It is at the layer 4 of OSI model.
-Protocol:It is at the layer 5 of OSI model.
-Protocol error:It is at the layer 6 of OSI model.
-Application:It is at the layer 6 of OSI model.
-Application data:It is at the layer 7 of OSI model.
